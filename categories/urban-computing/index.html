Profile

My research interests spanning over topics of history and politics of informatics in India, new media and technology studies, and data infrastructures and economies. I am also keenly interested in computational techniques in arts, humanities, and social research, and emerging methodological questions.

As the Research Director at the Centre for Internet and Society, I lead the Researchers at Work (RAW) programme and engage with policy and implementation works related to open data in particular and openness in general.

I am a member of DataMeet.
